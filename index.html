About Us

Our Aim

We are a youth group aiming to increase AI literacy based in London, UK. As AI continues to be implemented in our daily lives and industry sectors across the world, it is important that the next generation, who will be one of the first to experience the impacts of AI in the workplace, understand its benefits, flaws, and how best to combat future problems. We aim to create immersive, educational (and fun!) workshops and sessions for 10-18-year-olds across the UK, hosted by fellow young people, to raise awareness about AI.

Workshops

Responsible AI: A Policy Approach to Bias Eradication

As part of our aim to increase AI literacy among the youth we held “Responsible AI: A Policy Approach to Bias Eradication”, an interactive workshop under the AI Fringe where we taught participants about the importance of safe AI. In this interactive session, featuring guest speaker Matheace Ramautra, an AI Legal and Ethic Practitioner, we introduced the effects of AI biases on society, and potential ways to combat them through policy. Opening with a short presentation on AI biases and how it manifests in different sectors, our workshop was followed by an activity where groups will propose their own policies to mitigate them. There were many questions and open discussions.

Events

Truth vs Tech: Tackling Misinformation and Deepfakes

At the AI and Society Forum, we spoke at the AI and Society Forum on the panel “Truth vs Tech: Tackling Misinformation and Deepfakes”. The event held over 240 people and it was uplifting seeing so many people interested in our talk, despite being the youngest speakers and attendees of the event. Throughout the day, we heard talks from many people experienced in the field, from Abeba Birhane to Dr Maya Indira.
